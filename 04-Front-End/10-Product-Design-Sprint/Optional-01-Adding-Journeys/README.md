### Adding more Journeys

You are done with your Core User Journey, prototype and pitch preparation? Why not build another user journey in your prototype!

So far, we exclusively focused on the **most important** journey of your product: the Core User Journey. But the product you will build during the final weeks of the bootcamp will have more than one journey.

With the example of Airbnb, additional journeys could be:
- Canceling / Updating a booking
- Leaving a review

Or even journeys from the perspective of another *type of user*, the owner:
- Creating a new listing
- Updating the price per night
- Adding photos to your existing listing
